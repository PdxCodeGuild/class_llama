Welcome to the Burrito Bonanza!

Burrito Bonanza is a locally owned and locally sourced restuarant that hosts a build-a-burrito style menu. We sourced all of our ingredients from local farms and butchers. Our veggies come from Sunshine Valleys Farms inc. Our meats are delivered from Blackberry Butcheries off of Hwy 19. Our dairy products come from Hillerton and Sons Dairy farm. We pride ourselves on the freshness of our ingredients, we hope you enjoy your food!
