Visionary, Firebrand, Primarily Composed of Carbon

The life of Ser Langibus Witherspoon Twitwither XXXIV was one of great triumph over adversity. Having inherited a mere $8 billion dollars at the tender age of 32, he took courageous charge of the family business and managed to grow the estate to be valued at an unprecedented $50,000. Shortly after this resounding success, he was immolated by an enormous dragon and spent the next 100 years as the chairman of the board of Directors at a prestigious policy institute, overcoming the fact that he was now made entirely of ash. Twitwither was known for his citizenship in dozens of micronations around the world, including:

  • Molossia
  • Aeterna Lucina
  • British West Florida
  • Filettino
  • Melchizedek
  • Other World Kingdom
  • Zaqistan
It is rumored that Twitwither used the majority of his family's wealth to purchase citizenship in these nations, which he described as "really a great deal, I mean think of what you're getting. You're getting in on the ground floor of something real swell, just absolutely the best."

While Information about Chairman Twitwither is hard to find online, substantial information is available on Twitwither's closest childhood friend, Ronnie.
