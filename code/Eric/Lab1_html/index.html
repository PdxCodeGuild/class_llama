Pulp Ficiton: Marsellus Wallace

Bio:

Marsellus Wallace is a gang boss and husband to Mia Wallace.

He is the boss of Vincent Vega, Jules Winnfield, Butch Coolidge, and many other unknown gangsters.

He is said to have thrown a man off a building for giving Mia a foot massage.

Home Town: Los Angeles, California

Quote from Marsellus Wallace: "I ain't through wit you by a damn sight....I'm a get medieval on your a**!
